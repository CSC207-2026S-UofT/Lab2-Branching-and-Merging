# Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and ground cinnamon.
3. In another bowl, mix together the eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla.
4. Combine dry ingredients with wet ingredients, mixing until combined, but not overmixing.
5. Pour into 12" by 9" cake pan and let bake in oven for 40 minutes, or until a toothpick poked in the middle comes out clean.
6. Enjoy! :)

## Additional Directions by Bob:
4. Add a tablespoon of instant coffee to the dry ingredients for a hint of mocha flavor.
5. Mix in a handful of chocolate chunks for extra indulgence.
6. Line the bottom of the baking pan with parchment paper for easy cake removal.
